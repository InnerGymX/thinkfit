What might really be going on underneath your symptoms

Under the recurring pain, fatigue or anxiety, there may be:

  • A nervous system stuck in survival mode (fight, flight or freeze).
  • Unprocessed stress, grief, pressure or emotional load held in the body.
  • Beliefs like “I must always push harder” or “rest is laziness” driving burnout.

The Physiology of Stress

It’s not laziness. It’s biological protection.

When your mind is stressed, your body enters "survival mode." It holds onto fat, creates inflammation, and shuts down energy production to keep you safe.
